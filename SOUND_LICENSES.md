# Watch Party Soundboard Default Sounds

Default Watch Party soundboard sounds are local MP3 assets in `static/sounds/soundboard/`.

All listed source clips were downloaded from Mixkit and processed locally with FFmpeg for short duration, MP3 encoding, and loudness normalization.
