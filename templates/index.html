Setup Guide & Tutorial

1. How to use Cloudflare Tunnels (100% Free)

  1. You don't need any local setup! If you leave the **Cloudflare Tunnel Token** empty, AuraSort will automatically download `cloudflared.exe` and generate a temporary shareable URL for you when you run a Watch Party.
  2. If you want a persistent, custom domain (e.g. `watch.mywebsite.com`), log in to your **Cloudflare Dashboard**, navigate to **Access / Zero Trust** -> **Tunnels**, create a new tunnel, copy your **Tunnel Token**, and paste it above along with your domain name.

2. How to get a Free TURN Server (for Voice Chat)

  1. WebRTC voice chat needs a public relay server to cross NAT/firewalls.
  2. Go to [Metered.ca](https://www.metered.ca/) and sign up for a **Free Account** (includes 50GB of TURN data per month, enough for ~2,700 hours of talk time!).
  3. On your Metered dashboard, copy the **TURN Server URL**, **Username**, and **Password (Credential)**, and paste them in the fields above.

3. How to choose HLS Transcoding

  • **Toggle ON**: If your home upload bandwidth is limited. AuraSort will slice the video into 4-second segments. Viewers fetch only the current segment and Cloudflare's CDN caches them so that multiple viewers do not pull duplicate data from your machine.
  • **Toggle OFF (Default)**: Viewers stream the raw MP4 file directly from your computer. Playback starts instantly with no conversion overhead, but uses more upload speed per viewer.
